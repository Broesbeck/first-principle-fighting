Our Story

Where Discipline Becomes Freedom

First Principle Fighting was born in a Detroit warehouse with a simple belief: true freedom comes through mastery of body and mind.

We strip away the noise and return to what matters—fundamentals drilled until they become instinct, discipline practiced until it becomes character. Every rep counts. Every session builds.

This isn't a gym where you blend into the crowd. It's a tribe that pushes you, holds you accountable, and celebrates your growth. We show up for each other, every day.

Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or you've been fighting for years, you'll find a community that meets you where you are—and refuses to let you stay there.
