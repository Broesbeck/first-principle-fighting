Our Programs

Every program at First Principle Fighting is built on the same foundation: master the fundamentals, embrace the process, earn your progress. Whether you're new to martial arts or a seasoned competitor, you'll find instruction that meets you where you are—and pushes you beyond.

Ground Game

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

The gentle art that isn't gentle at all. BJJ is chess with consequences—a system of leverage, control, and submissions that lets a smaller person defeat a larger opponent through technique and strategy.

What to Expect

Technical drilling, positional sparring, and live rolling. Every class builds on fundamentals while introducing new concepts. Gi and no-gi classes available.

Who It's For

Complete beginners to advanced competitors. All experience levels train together—you'll learn from everyone on the mat.

Start Training

Stand Up

Muay Thai

The art of eight limbs. Muay Thai uses fists, elbows, knees, and shins to create a complete striking system. It's direct, efficient, and devastatingly effective.

What to Expect

Pad work, heavy bag rounds, technique drilling, and controlled sparring. Build power, timing, and the cardio to go the distance.

Who It's For

Anyone looking to develop striking skills—whether for fitness, self-defense, or competition. No experience required.

Start Training

Sweet Science

Boxing

Footwork. Head movement. The jab. Boxing strips striking down to its purest form—just your hands, your feet, and your mind. Simple to learn, infinite to master.

What to Expect

Shadow boxing, mitt work, heavy bag combinations, and technical sparring. Build rhythm, timing, and defensive instincts.

Who It's For

Perfect for beginners—boxing fundamentals translate to every combat sport. Also ideal for those focused purely on hand skills.

Start Training

Complete Fighter

Mixed Martial Arts

Where all disciplines merge. MMA integrates striking, grappling, and transitions into one adaptive system. Learn to fight anywhere the fight goes.

What to Expect

Integration drills combining striking and grappling, cage work, takedown defense, and controlled MMA sparring.

Who It's For

Recommended after building fundamentals in at least one discipline. For those who want to become complete martial artists.

Start Training

Future Warriors

Kids Program

Discipline, respect, and confidence—built through martial arts fundamentals. Our kids program focuses on character as much as technique, creating strong young people on and off the mat.

What to Expect

Age-appropriate training in BJJ and striking basics. Games, drills, and structured learning in a supportive environment.

Who It's For

Ages 6-14. No experience needed. Classes grouped by age and skill level.
